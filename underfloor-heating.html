WunderFloor Hydronic Underfloor Heating

WunderFloor is a specialised division of our company, dedicated to providing bespoke hydronic underfloor heating solutions. We bring the same commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction to this niche service, ensuring your project benefits from the most advanced and efficient underfloor heating technology.

Key Aspects of WunderFloor Systems

How It Works

Hydronic underfloor heating circulates warm water through pipes beneath the floor surface, providing radiant heat that warms the room from the ground up.

Installation

Our expert technicians handle the complete installation process, ensuring precise pipe layout, proper insulation, and seamless integration with your flooring.

Running Costs

WunderFloor systems are designed for optimal energy efficiency, resulting in lower running costs compared to traditional heating methods.
